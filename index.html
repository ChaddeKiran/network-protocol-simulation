TCP (Transmission Control Protocol)

TCP is a connection-oriented protocol that guarantees reliable and ordered delivery of data. It uses a three-way handshake (SYN, SYN-ACK, ACK) to establish a connection before data transfer, and ensures all packets are received in the correct order through sequence numbers and acknowledgments.
